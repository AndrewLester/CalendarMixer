Schoology Data We Store

In order to provide quick and efficient access to your calendar, we need to store some of your personal schoology data on our own servers. All specific information is listed below, along with its usage for your experience:

User info:

  1. User ID: Identifies your account from an external application attempting to access your calendar. (e.x. Google Calendar). Does not provide authentication
  2. Username: Used for filtering purposes
  3. Email: Used for email notifications on Calendar Alarms
  4. Timezone: Used for displaying the correct Date image favicon as well as setting the exported Calendar's timezone

Course info:

  2. Name:
  3. Realm name:

Schoology Data We Access

Most of the Schoology Data we use is only viewed on a per request basis. This means that it is not stored by our website's persistent storage, but it may exist in a user-specific cache for a short amount of time.

Realm Identifiers (Groups, Courses, Schools, etc.)

  1. Used on the client to provide options for filtering. Only selected items in a filter are stored in persistent storage

Calendar Event Information (Title, Description, etc.)
  1. Used on the client to display the user's calendar. The only piece of information that might be stored permanently is an Event's ID if it is part of an Alert
